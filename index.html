About us

Book It is a web service that makes reserving recreational facilities easy. Gone are the days where you need to hassle over reserving a spot, now you don't need to worry if you have a spot for your scrimmage, practice, or workout. Book It makes it simple to see when there are spots open and you can even claim a time. Book It also gives a platform to private facility owners to advertise and rent out their spaces.
